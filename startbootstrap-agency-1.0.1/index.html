Nutshell

What is Campus BookFlip?

Buy it

Everyone in college needs textbooks; you can't escape it. Why not buy your textbooks quickly, affordably, and conveniently?

Read it

Keep your textbooks for however long you need to; they're your's.

Flip it

Ace your final exams, and then sell your textbooks at the price they originally cost you!

About

How does Campus BookFlip work?

  • Step 1

    Register

    Sign up for your account with us, and link your college to your account.

  • Step 2

    List Your Books

    If you have textbooks you no longer need, start selling them right away. Other people on campus will be able to see the books you're selling and buy them directly from you.

  • Step 3

    Find the Books You Need

    Search for textbooks listed under your college and buy them from your fellow classmates in person. If you don't find what you're looking for on campus, we provide you with a link to buy it on Amazon.

  • Step 4

    Be the Early Bird

    List your newly purchased textbooks immediately, and specify when they'll be available for purchase. That way, others will know what you'll be selling all semester long.

  • Step 5

    Spread the Word!

    Show your support for us by telling your friends about this awesome service built by college kids for college kids. Be sure to mention that it's free!

How we're different

Just like most college kids, we love Amazon. Most likely, they'll remain as a great method of acquiring your textbooks for a long time. The problem is once you buy your books from them, they're no longer interested in your books - nor should they be. They're in the business of selling.
